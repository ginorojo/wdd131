Hungry appe

I chose this name because it's the name of my sister's restaurant, and since she doesn't have an informative website for her business, I decided to create a website for her restaurant. The name "Appe" is a cryptocurrency Appe coin, and my brother-in-law has an NFT of that coin which he uses as the brand logo.

Site purpose

Hungry appe is a real restaurant which belongs to my sister, this restaurant offers different varieties of lunch at a very affordable price and with an amazing quality, in addition to fast food. All this with Peruvian seasoning.

Scenarios

  • Where can I have delicius lunch today at an affordable price?
  • Where can I eat Peruvian food made by Peruvians?
